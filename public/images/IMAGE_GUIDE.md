## To Swap Images

Simply replace the files in `/public/images/` with your preferred images, keeping the same filenames:
- `epk-cover.png` - Hero portrait
- `hero-1.png` - Featured tiles
- `og-image.png` - Social preview

Or update the component files to reference different image paths.
